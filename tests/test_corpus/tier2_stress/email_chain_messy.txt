Ravi,

Workaround for the CH3 noise: bump the attenuation 2 steps (+6dB) and increase the integration time from 4 to 8 sweeps. You'll lose some sensitivity but the noise floor should come back within spec. I used the same trick at Driftwood last month when we had a similar module failure.

Part numbers you need:
  AB-115 Rev B (amplifier card) - qty 1
  FM-220 (filter module) - qty 2, the ones on CH3 looked corroded when I was there in January

Put in the PO ASAP, these cards have been on 6-week lead time.

-Paul
