3. sensor system Calibration Procedure
The sensor system calibration procedure at Thule uses a GPS-disciplined reference oscillator as the timing reference.
Technicians connect the calibration signal generator, align timing to UTC via GPS, and verify ionogram quality after each adjustment.
Calibration is not accepted until the ionogram quality returns to nominal trace clarity and frequency alignment.
